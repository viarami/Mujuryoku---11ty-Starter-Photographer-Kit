Consistency is one of the fastest ways to make a portfolio feel professional.

## 1. Start with a base preset

Create one neutral preset for your preferred style. Keep contrast and saturation moderate so you can adapt per scene.

## 2. Correct exposure before color

Always fix exposure and white balance before touching creative color tools. Otherwise, the same preset can produce very different results.

## 3. Edit in scene groups

Batch images by lighting scenario:

- Outdoor direct sun
- Outdoor shade
- Indoor mixed light
- Flash-lit events

This keeps adjustments small and repeatable.

## 4. Compare images side by side

Use grid or compare view during final pass. If one image jumps out too much, it likely needs tonal adjustment.

## 5. Export for context

Export a test set and review it on desktop and mobile. Consistency should hold across screens.

A clean, repeatable editing process saves hours and makes your style recognizable.
